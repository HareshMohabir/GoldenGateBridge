Visiting the world famous Golden Gate Bridge is a unique experience for everyone. Whether you hike, walk, bike, shop, take a guided tour, or just sit back and take it all in, there is something for everyone. The Golden Gate bridge has many exhibits and you can walk around be toured for free thanks to a non-profit organization associated with the San Francisco Public Library.
